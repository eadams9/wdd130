Ghana

Ghana is a country located in West Africa. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful landscapes. Ghana is home to many historical sites, including the Cape Coast Castle and the Elmina Castle, which were used during the transatlantic slave trade. The country is also famous for its music, dance, and colorful festivals. Ghana's economy is primarily based on agriculture, mining, and oil production. The official language of Ghana is English, and the currency is the Ghanaian cedi.
