Featured Trips

Los Dientes del Diablo

Take on one of the most legendary stretches of river in the region. Class IV–V rapids demand skill and nerve; our guides will get you through safely and with stories to tell.

  • Difficulty: Class IV–V
  • Duration: Full Day
  • Min Age: 16

Big Mallard

Big Mallard is a fan favorite; a long, rolling Class III rapid with enough punch to thrill without overwhelming. Perfect for intermediate paddlers looking for their next challenge.

  • Difficulty: Class III
  • Duration: Half Day
  • Min Age: 12

Half Day Adventure

New to rafting? This is your perfect introduction. Calm stretches and gentle Class II rapids make this an ideal family outing with beautiful scenery all the way through.

  • Difficulty: Class II
  • Duration: Half Day
  • Min Age: 6
